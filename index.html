About Us

We take pride in delivering thorough and careful boat restoration and cleaning services, using proven marine techniques to deliver long-lasting, professional results.

We are primarily serving the Bristol and Portishead marinas, however as a mobile service we will be happy to provide quotes for jobs further away if requested.

Whether it's a quick scrub or full detail, we use trusted marine products and proven techniques to keep your boat looking its best.

If you have any questions or would like to make a booking please send us a message and we'll get back to you.
